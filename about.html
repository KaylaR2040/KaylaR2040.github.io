The Bridgerton 'About'

Lady Whistledown

Ah, dearest reader, how the whispers do fly! While my identity remains a mystery, allow me to reveal just a touch of the intrigue surrounding me and my connections.

I share a love for travel, much like the ladies of Bridgerton, and once even journeyed across Europe with a most unusual companion—my pet snail, who braved airport security by my side. Oh, the tales that snail could tell!

As for my family, well, let's just say there are connections in high places. However, I shall leave the specifics to your imagination. And while I must remain coy, I can share that another member of my family, my grandmother, once soared through the skies, piloting a plane at the tender age of 8—a tale of daring worthy of high society’s finest.

Now, shifting our gaze to you, dear Odera. Though not originally from these parts, you bring with you the grace and charm of your hometown, Charlotte, NC. The whispers suggest that you are destined for greatness within our sisterhood. We cannot help but anticipate your shining moment and the wonderful addition you will make to our family. Queen Charlotte herself would be proud of such a radiant young woman representing her namesake city.

So, dear Odera, though I may remain a mystery for now, I have no doubt that your future will shine as brightly as you do. The stage is set, and we are all eagerly awaiting your grand entrance into our family.

Yours ever faithfully,
Lady Whistledown
